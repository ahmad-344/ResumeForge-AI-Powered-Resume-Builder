Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Effective immediately

🔒 Short version: ResumeForge is a browser-based application. Your resume data is stored only in your browser's memory and is never sent to our servers. We do not collect, store, or sell any personal data.

1 Who We Are

ResumeForge ("we," "us," or "our") is a free, browser-based resume building tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our application works. This Privacy Policy explains what information is handled when you use ResumeForge.

2 Information We Do NOT Collect

Because ResumeForge operates entirely within your browser, we do not collect:

  • Resume content — Your name, contact details, work history, education, skills, and all other resume data exist only in your browser's memory while the app is open.
  • Account information — No registration or login is required. We have no user accounts, no passwords, and no user database.
  • Uploaded files — When you import a resume (PDF, DOCX, or TXT), the file is read locally in your browser. It is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Payment information — ResumeForge is completely free. No payment data is collected.
  • Cookies or tracking — We do not use advertising cookies, analytics trackers, or cross-site tracking technologies.

3 AI Features and Third-Party API

If you choose to use AI-powered features (summary generation, bullet points, cover letter writing, etc.), these features require an API key from xAI (Grok). Please note:

  • Your API key is stored locally in your browser's localStorage only. It is sent directly from your browser to xAI's servers when you use AI features — it never passes through ResumeForge servers.
  • Content sent to AI: The specific text you enter for AI generation (job title, skills, context) is transmitted to xAI's API to generate a response. This is governed by xAI's Privacy Policy.
  • No AI logging by us: We do not log, store, or review any content sent to the AI or responses received from it.
  • AI features are entirely optional. All core resume building features work without any API key.

4 Data Storage in Your Browser

ResumeForge uses your browser's localStorage to store only your API key (if you choose to save it). Your resume data exists in JavaScript memory while the app is open and is lost when you close the browser tab.

You can clear your stored API key at any time by clicking the "AI Key" button and selecting "Clear," or by clearing your browser's localStorage/site data.

5 Google Fonts

ResumeForge uses fonts (Sora and Nunito Sans) loaded from Google Fonts. When these fonts load, your browser makes a request to Google's servers, which may log your IP address per Google's standard practices. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. No font data is sent to us.

6 Children's Privacy

ResumeForge is intended for users who are 16 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has used our service, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7 Your Rights

Since we do not collect your personal data, there is no data for us to access, correct, export, or delete on our end. You have full control over your data because it lives entirely in your browser. You can:

  • Close the browser tab to clear all resume data from memory.
  • Clear your browser's site data to remove any stored API key.
  • Use the app in private/incognito mode for zero local persistence.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of ResumeForge after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about this Privacy Policy?

We're committed to privacy and transparency. If you have any questions or concerns about how ResumeForge handles data, please feel free to reach out.
